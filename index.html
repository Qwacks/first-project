The Tropic of Cancer

My favorite book is The Tropic of Cancer by Henry Miller. I chose this one because it is the book I last read. It's a statement about freedom of speech, and was banned from the U.S. for a long while after it was published. I enjoyed the book for it's classical feel mixed with a content and style that makes it feel so alive.


  • Chicken Burrito


    I am a burrito man. This large tortilla filled with salsa, beans, cilantro lime rice, and chicken is a great meal if you're on the go or just want to avoid washing utensils.


  • Pineapple Fried Rice

    Fried rice is always good, and whenever I get thai food I always ask for Pineapple Fried Rice. This goes fantastically with the generally spicy thai classics, and is easy to cook at home.


  • Grilled Cheese

    Practically the easiest thing to make besides PB and J, grilling your sandwhich brings unlimited options to the table. Tomatoes, bacon, onions and garlic. You can add anything to enhance this classic.
